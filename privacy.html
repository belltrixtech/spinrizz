Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

SpinRizz ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Email address and password when you create an account.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the app, including spins, chip balance, streaks, and saved lines.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers.
  • Purchase Information: Records of in-app purchases processed through Apple's App Store. We do not store your payment details.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the SpinRizz app
  • Track your chip balance, streak, and saved lines across devices
  • Process in-app purchases and restore them when needed
  • Send push notifications (only if you opt in)
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve the app experience
  • Respond to support requests

3. Third-Party Services

SpinRizz uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Firebase (Google): For user authentication and data storage. See Firebase Privacy Policy.
  • OpenAI: For AI-generated pickup lines. Prompts are sent to OpenAI's API but are not linked to your personal identity. See OpenAI Privacy Policy.
  • Apple App Store: For payment processing and subscription management.

4. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time through the Profile screen in the app or by contacting us directly.

5. Children's Privacy

SpinRizz is intended for users aged 17 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 17. If we become aware that a child under 17 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it promptly.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt out of push notifications at any time through your device settings

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
